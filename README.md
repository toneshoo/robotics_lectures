All programs were delivered in-person to real participants, with working hardware and live demonstrations.

About

These programs were developed and led by the Aquadrag Robotics Club (DRAG SA) at KAU, under the Mawhiba national gifted student program and through independent club initiatives.

The curriculum spans:


Embedded systems (Arduino, ESP32)
Mechanical design and fabrication
Electrical systems and PCB basics
Software: ROS2, finite state machines, autonomous navigation
Competitive robotics strategy and teamwork
